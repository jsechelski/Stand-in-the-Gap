The Journey to Stand in the Gap

The Mission:

Mission Goal: Supporting those who aren’t able to support themselves.  Giving selflessly. Doing more than we are asked. Fighting for those who are in a dark place in their life and need someone else to fight for them.  Helping brothers and sisters who are impacted by the effects of substance abuse and haven’t found their way just yet.


I know what it means to be with someone who is addicted to something that can transform who they are in front of your very eyes.  Several people I love have had struggles with different types of substances. Also, my body became addicted to Vyvanse, an ADHD medication, for about a year and a half. Only the taking the prescribed dose my doctor gave me. I know the ability for something to grab a hold of you so quickly and transform you.  That it can change the way you respond to people & situations. It literally was controlling my actions. I would catch myself thinking, why are you acting like this or getting upset over this? It was changing me & it was eye opening to realize and gain an understanding of things. My own experience gave me a insight to the struggle other people go through. I am mentally strong and pay attention to my body. I was able to notice the changes it was causing in myself. I don’t know if others are able to do so or if they have someone who is able to recognize and be honest with them about what they are dealing with.

Most individuals have some type of addiction.  Yes, possibly even you.

What’s your drug of choice?  Sugar, medication, money, alcohol, food, sex, gambling, working out, spoiling your children, video games, hunting/fishing, shopping, caffeine/coffee, crack, extreme sports, this list goes on…

The truth is some have greater negative consequences than others, however, it is the same struggle when it comes down to it.  If your body goes through withdraws of anything it has gained an attachment or addiction to it. Call it what you want. The chemistry in your brain is fueled by these things and how they make you feel. They all lead us to a direction of depending on other objects, people, statuses, etc. more than we should.  Our bodies begin to make adjustments for everything we put into it. This is why it’s so important to feed your body, mind and soul with positive things.

Our desire is to assist people in finding outlets to cope with these things as they working to get back to themselves without enabling them to continue destructive lifestyles. We will achieve this by building a mental health facility in our community. It will have a gym, a yoga studio, an art studio, a children's activity center, parent education rooms, rooms to assist Veterans with PTSD, rooms to hold Al-anon meetings, and nutrition counseling rooms. All of these will allow people in our community the outlets to be educated and also have the facilities to put these things into action in their lives. It is very misunderstood and also something that is avoided. It affects all races, social statuses & gender. It is a very real & large problem in our communities coast to coast.

We appreciate your consideration to join this mission in whatever way possible.  We have a desire to work with those who support our goals as well! ☺
